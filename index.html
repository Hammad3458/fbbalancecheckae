What Is FAB Salary & Account Balance Check?

FAB balance check allows customers to view:

  • Available balance (money you can use immediately)
  • Ledger balance (total balance including pending transactions)
  • Salary credits
  • Recent transactions
  • Card status (active, blocked, expired)

FAB provides balance inquiry services through digital, offline, and assisted channels, ensuring that every customer can access their financial information easily.

Who Can Use FAB Balance Check Services?

  • Salary account holders
  • Ratibi card users
  • FAB prepaid card holders
  • Savings and current account customers
  • Credit card users
  • UAE residents and international FAB customers

You do not need advanced technical knowledge to check your FAB balance. Many methods work without internet access.

Method 1: FAB Salary & Balance Check Using FAB Mobile App

The FAB Mobile App is the most complete and convenient option in 2026. It provides real-time balance updates and full banking features.

Steps to Check Balance via FAB Mobile App

  • Download the official FAB Mobile App from Google Play or Apple App Store
  • Open the app and tap Log In
  • Sign in using Customer ID, debit or credit card number, or registered username
  • Complete OTP or biometric verification
  • Your balance appears instantly on the home screen

Why the FAB Mobile App Is Best

  • Real-time salary and balance updates
  • Full transaction history
  • Statement downloads
  • High security with biometrics
  • Available 24/7

Method 2: FAB Salary Balance Check via SMS

FAB SMS Banking is ideal for users without smartphones or internet access.

How to Check FAB Balance by SMS

  • Open your phone’s SMS app
  • Send message to 2121
  • Type BAL followed by the last four digits of your account number

You will receive available balance, ledger balance, and last transaction summary.

Method 3: FAB Salary & Account Balance Check via ATM

  • Insert your FAB debit card
  • Enter your ATM PIN
  • Select language
  • Choose Balance Inquiry
  • View balance on screen or print receipt

This method works 24/7 at FAB ATMs across the UAE.

Method 4: FAB Balance Check via Online Banking Website

  • Visit the official FAB website
  • Click Login and select Personal Online Banking
  • Enter username and password
  • Verify OTP
  • View balances and transactions on the dashboard

Method 5: FAB Salary Balance Check via Phone Banking

  • UAE: 600 52 5500
  • International: +971 2 681 1511

Phone banking is suitable for customers who prefer voice assistance.

Method 6: FAB Ratibi & Salary Balance Check via Payit App

  • Download the Payit app
  • Register using Emirates ID and UAE mobile number
  • Link your Ratibi or prepaid card
  • View your salary balance instantly

Method 7: FAB Balance Check at Branch

  • Visit the nearest FAB branch
  • Carry Emirates ID and account or card details
  • Get assistance from bank staff

Is FAB Balance Check Safe in 2026?

Customers should never share PINs or OTPs.
